Disaster Resource Preparation

Q: How do I navigate around the application?

A: At the top left area of the screen there is a menu that allows you to navigate to other areas of the application. You can also access this menu by sliding left to right of the screen.

Q: Is there a way to back track from the distaster pages?

A: Yes, you can only go backward by using the devices back button.

Q: How can I share this information with my friends?

A: As of right now, there is no share button implemented on the application, though; you can take screen shots with your device and email the image to whoever you want to share the information. Once the application is uploaded to the Google Play store, then you will be able to share the application link.

Q: Where can I get the resources listed in the disaster resource kit?

A: Many of the items listed in the disaster resource kit can be purchased at your local retail store, such as a Walmart. Other items, like tools, can be found at a Home Depot or Lowes.

Q: How do I contact the developers if I have a question or want to submit feedback?

A:There is a "contact us" page within the application that allows you to send us an email of your questions and/or feedback. You can also use the contact page to send us your thoughts.
